Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe

The best cookies. Hands down.


Ingredients You'll Need

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Stir together peanut butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla extract in a large bowl until combined.
  2. Shape dough into 1-inch balls. Place balls 1 inch apart on ungreased baking sheets, and flatten gently with tines of a fork.
  3. Bake at 325Â°F for 15 minutes or until golden brown. Remove to wire racks to cool.
